addGuide1() sets the display of the element with an id of #ecampusGuide to flex and adds a class of .Guide1 to the item with an id of #ecampusContainer. 

removeGuide1AddGuid2() removes Guide1 and adds Guide 2 instead. 

removeGuide2AddGuid3 removes Guide2 and adds Guide 3 instead. 

removeLastClass() removes Guide 3 and sets the display of the element with an id of #ecampusGuide to none
